# ClearFlow solutions (HDS)
### Our premise:
We are a team of highly dedicated individuals who want to revolutionize the drain cleaning industry.
We have created a product which can compete effectively with other giants in the space fo a huge section of the market.
It is cheap and cost effective.

### Our design
Our design consists of a simple turnbuckle screw which can be used to adjust the length of the rod to fit any drain imaginable.
The current prototype is 3d printed and has No moving parts which makes it simple and easy to build.
Our manufacturing costs are as low as 1600 rupees and can be reduced further with the help of efficient manufacturing.
We plan on presenting our product to La Fondation Dassault Systemes for the STFE - Seed the future entrepreneurs program 2025.

### Roadmap
We have completed product ideation, design and manufacturing. The only thing left for us to do now is present it to the judges and effectively compete
with the competition.
